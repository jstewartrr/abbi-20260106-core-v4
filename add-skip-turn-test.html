Add Skip Turn Tool to ABBI

What this does:

Adds the skip_turn system tool to ABBI (abbi-openai test agent).

This enables ABBI to automatically pause and stay silent when you say:

  • "wait" / "hold on"
  • "I need a moment" / "just a moment"
  • "give me a second" / "one moment"
  • "let me think" / "hold that thought"
